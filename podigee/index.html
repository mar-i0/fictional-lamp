A modern web player. For podcasts. From Podigee.

HTML5 only

… allows playback on all kinds of devices from Smartphone to Desktop.

Themeable

… to show your own style or choose from premade themes.

Extensible

… to keep up with the ever evolving podcast industry.

Responsive

… making it look good on all screen sizes from small to large.

Extensions

Chaptermarks

… let the listener jump directly to a specific part of the episode.

Chromecast (experimental)

… makes your podcast ready for the big screen.

Downloads

… allow downloading the episode to the device.

Episode information

… shows further information like shownotes.

Playlist

… lets the listener explore your podcast's back catalog.

Sharing

… makes sharing an episode easy as cake.

Transcripts

… enable easier finding, citing and sharing.
